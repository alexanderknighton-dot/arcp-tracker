Export your portfolio from the RCOG ePortfolio as a PDF and drop it here. Everything is processed locally in your browser — no data leaves your device.

How to export from ePortfolio

  1. Log in to eportfolio.rcog.org.uk
  2. Go to My Portfolio → Export
  3. Select Full export as PDF and download
  4. Drop the PDF on the box above

🔒 Your PDF is read entirely in your browser using PDF.js. Nothing is uploaded to any server.
